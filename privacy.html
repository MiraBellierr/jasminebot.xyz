While using Our Bot, We may automatically collect Your User information such as your tag, your username, your user ID and etc to provide Us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify You via Discord and for the functionality of certain commands.


Guild Information

While using Our Bot, We may automatically collect your Guild information such as the name, the owner, member count and etc that can be used to identify and for the functionality of certain commands.

The Company may use the Data for the following purposes:
